The Mist (2007) is hands down my favorite horror movie. It has an intense level of mystery that keeps you in the dark. The foggy atmosphere of the movie is perfectly unsettling. The main soundtrack, which I forgot the name of, is beautifully eerie. The immersion is unforgettable too. 

This movie is a certified hood classic.
